# NOTICE – Daniela Alphabet

This communication system originates from the real-life experience of Daniela Gazzano.

It has been created to restore communication, dignity, and human connection for people living with severe paralysis.

This system is declared a humanitarian common good.

Any attempt to:
- commercialize
- privatize
- patent
- or restrict access

goes against its fundamental purpose.

The world is invited to use it freely, responsibly, and ethically.

"Some languages are not invented. They are born from necessity."

Daniela Alphabet

A communication system for those who cannot speak

Free Universal Non-commercial Human Right
Daniela Alphabet enables communication through minimal signals such as eye blinks. Developed from real-life experience, it restores dignity, connection and human presence where spoken language is no longer possible.
“A blink can say everything.”
This system is given freely to humanity.

It belongs to everyone and to no one.
It cannot be owned, patented or used for profit.

Its only purpose is to restore communication where there is none.

Human Blink Communication System

Daniela Alphabet

A communication system for those who cannot speak.

Free Universal Non-commercial Human

Daniela Alphabet allows communication through eye blinks and minimal signals. Born from the real-life experience of Daniela Gazzano, it was created to restore dignity, connection and human presence where spoken language is no longer possible.

“A blink can say everything.”
